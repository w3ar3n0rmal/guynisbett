AI - A Brutalist Sculpture Show

Written By Guy Nisbett

Welcome to the private view of my first sculpture show, at The Barbican in London… with a little help from AI.

In the heart of the Barbican Estate is the typically hard to find ‘Sculpture Court’. Next to the disused original entrance to the art gallery, it was designed for open air sculpture exhibitions, but has no sculptures. This ‘Brutalist Sculpture Show’ series uses photography and AI to imagine what could have been.
